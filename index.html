Flood mapping

In response to the devastating flooding in Kenya between mid-April and May 2024, I utilized Sentinel-1 radar technology to analyze the flood extent along the Tana River, one of the most severely affected areas. Leveraging Google Earth Engine and QGIS, I mapped approximately 14 square kilometers of inundated regions and conducted a preliminary damage assessment.

Change Detection

I embarked on an analysis to detect and monitor the shoreline changes of Lake Baringo, a vital freshwater lake in Kenya, from 2016-2023. This project leveraged the Normalized Difference Water Index (NDWI) to accurately delineate water bodies and track their fluctuations.

Precision Agriculture

This project leverages advanced geospatial technologies, including drone imagery and GIS, to enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability. By integrating high-resolution data on soil health, crop conditions, and farm boundaries, the project enables precise monitoring, analysis, and management of agricultural resources. The goal is to optimize farm inputs, reduce waste, and improve yield, ultimately supporting data-driven decision-making for better farm management.
